Apu Basketball Clubs

 What is our purpose?

To provide a Basketball environment where personal development in basketball skills and fitness is encouraged in a team situation, with a strong emphasis on continually developing player attitude and character consistent with Biblical principles.

* To promote an increased awareness both within and outside the Club of basketball activities to young people.

 * To gather together people interested in the overall development of the basketball activities.

Vision of our club

The vision and purpose of the Basketball Association of APU (ABC) is to contribute to the physical and social well-being of the youth. It is a promotion and development of mutual understanding, harmony and tolerance.
 
The objective is to provide the youth a forum to participate in an educational exchange among student-athletes all over the regions, harness their talents through sports activities on their potentials for provincial, regional and national competitions.

Our Mission

The Basketball Association of APU (ABC) mission is to develop and support the different league-organizations, institutions, clubs and corporate groups that take part in our national competitions. Moreover, it seeks to promote basketball throughout the different regions by way of engaging in various activities such as seminar, the technical aspect of the tournament and basketball clinic.
