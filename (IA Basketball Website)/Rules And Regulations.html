RULES AND REGULATION OF BASKETBALL




What Are Basketball's Rules?
  • Only five players per team are allowed on the floor.

  • To win, you must score more goals than your opponent.

  • Score while the shot clock is still running.

  • The ball is advanced by dribbling.

  • Five seconds are given to the offensive to inbound the ball.

  • The ball must be advanced by the offensive.

  • The ball and ball handler must stay inside the boundary.

  • The ball may be legally blocked or stolen by defenders.

  • After three seconds, defenders must leave the paint.

  • A fixed number of five fouls are allocated to each team per quarter.

  • There is a foul for unwarranted contact.

  • A flagrant foul is called when there is excessive contact.

  • An offensive foul is committed when there are charges or illegal screens.

  • Technical fouls are given for certain rule violations.
